The International Space Station (ISS)

The International Space Station is a science laboratory that orbits Earth, built through global collaboration between space agencies like NASA, ESA, and others. It travels around the planet every 90 minutes, allowing astronauts to perform experiments in microgravity that benefit life on Earth and prepare for future missions to the Moon and Mars.
